Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to commonly asked questions regarding reservations, private transfers, and touring packages.

Our tour packages are completely customizable. You can request changes to hotel tiers, add sightseeing stops, extend travel days, or select specific private cars on our booking page. Enter your custom requests in the 'Special Requirements' field on the booking form.

We use three distinct categories: Deluxe Tier (premium 3-star heritage hotels), Premium Tier (handpicked 4-star properties and wellness resorts), and Royal Luxury Tier (exquisite 5-star properties and palaces, e.g., Taj, Oberoi, and boutique heritage resorts).

Intercity flights mentioned explicitly in the itineraries (e.g. Mumbai to Goa, or Hyderabad to Chennai flights) are fully included. Inbound and outbound flights to / from the starting and ending airport terminals are excluded and must be arranged independently.

Standard tour bookings can be cancelled for a full refund (less a 5% handling fee) up to 14 days before departure. Cancellations made between 7 and 13 days receive a 50% refund. Cancellations under 7 days are non-refundable due to hotel commitments. Detailed policies are specified on each package details page.
